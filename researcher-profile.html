About

Dr. Sarah Chen is a leading researcher in computational biology and machine learning applications in genomics. With over 10 years of experience, she has pioneered novel approaches to drug discovery using AI-driven methods. Her work has been published in top-tier journals including Nature, Science, and Cell.

As both a Principal Investigator and Research Consultant, Dr. Chen collaborates with pharmaceutical companies and academic institutions to accelerate breakthrough discoveries in personalized medicine and therapeutic development.
