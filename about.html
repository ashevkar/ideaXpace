Our Mission

To accelerate scientific discovery by making research funding accessible, transparent, and community-driven. We believe that breakthrough innovations shouldn't be limited by traditional funding barriers.

Accessible

Breaking down barriers to research funding for scientists worldwide

Transparent

Complete visibility into how funds are used and research progress

Community-Driven

Empowering supporters to directly impact scientific advancement

Our Vision

A world where scientific curiosity and innovation are never constrained by funding limitations. We envision a future where every promising research idea has the opportunity to flourish, supported by a global community that values discovery and progress.

By 2030, we aim to:

Fund $1 billion in research projects globally

Support 10,000+ research projects

Connect 1 million research supporters

Operate in every country worldwide

Our Journey

From a simple idea to a global platform supporting thousands of researchers worldwide.

2019 - The Beginning

Founded

ResearchFund was founded by Dr. Rachel Martinez and James Chen with the vision of democratizing research funding. Our first office was a small co-working space in San Francisco.

2020 - First Million

Milestone

We reached our first $1 million in funded research projects, supporting 50 researchers across 15 countries. The platform gained recognition in the scientific community.

2021 - Team Expansion

Growth

Our team grew to 25 members, and we opened our second office in Boston. We launched Lab Notes, our research blog, to better connect researchers with supporters.

2022 - Global Expansion

International

We expanded internationally, supporting researchers in over 50 countries. Our platform processed over $10 million in research funding and gained 50,000 active supporters.

2023 - Innovation & Impact

Present

Today, we've funded over $50 million in research across 2,500+ projects. Our AI-powered matching system helps connect researchers with the most relevant supporters, and we continue to innovate in the research funding space.
